How does this application work?

Farmer crop recovery allows farmers to obtain a rewarding through Algorand blockchain after reaching ten days of drought or intense rain.

There are two types of drought and three types of intense rain conditions that are illustrated below:

• Agricultural drought: temperature higher than a defined threshold and humidity lower than a defined threshold for ten consecutive days. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.agri_drought }} ALGO

• Meteorological drought: rain mm lower than a defined threshold for ten consecutive days. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.meteo_drought }} ALGO

• Heavy rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than a defined threshold. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.heavy_rain }} ALGO

• Shower rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than a defined threshold. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.shower_rain }} ALGO

• Storm rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than a defined threshold. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.storm_rain }} ALGO

How should I proceed?

• Register farm locations

• Register farmers

• View transaction requests and decide either to accept or reject them

• You can update rewardings basing on the current ALGO/EUR rate.
