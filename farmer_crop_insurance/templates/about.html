Farmer crop recovery

About this application

Farmer crop recovery allows you to obtain a rewarding through Algorand blockchain after reaching ten days of drought or intense rain.

There are two types of drought and three types of intense rain conditions that are illustrated below:

• Agricultural drought: temperature higher than {{ user.temperature_threshold }} °C and humidity lower than {{ user.humidity_threshold }} % for ten consecutive days. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.agri_drought }} ALGO

• Meteorological drought: rain mm lower than {{ user.rain_lower }} for ten consecutive days. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.meteo_drought }} ALGO

• Heavy rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than {{ user.rain_heavy }}. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.heavy_rain }} ALGO

• Shower rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than {{ user.rain_shower }}. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.shower_rain }} ALGO

• Storm rain: rain mm mean of last ten days is higher than {{ user.rain_storm }}. Actual rewarding: {{ rewarding.storm_rain }} ALGO

Rewardings are updated basing on the current ALGO/EUR rate. {% endblock %}
